Concept

The Japanese clothing market has been hit hard by the pandemic phenomenon due to the coronavirus. Thus, Japan is living a different life than before the COVID-19 era. As a result, the size of the market for purchasing clothes offline has decreased significantly, and most of them prefer online shopping. I wanted to use the crisis of the current situation as an opportunity. How should I prepare for this graduation work so that many people can see it? I started preparing with this question. Have you ever thought that there was no computer in the global era of the 21st century? Currently, we are living in a world full of irreplaceable computers and mobile devices. According to the times, I am releasing a web page to express my values and worldviews. This is NSFT based on a more novel and creative concept of "3D and sportswear".
